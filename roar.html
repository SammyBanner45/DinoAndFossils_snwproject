The eras of the Colossals:
Era Period Time Span (Million Years Ago) Key Features
Mesozoic Triassic 252 - 201 First dinosaurs and mammals appeared; dry climate with deserts.
Mesozoic Jurassic 201 - 145 Age of giant sauropods; lush vegetation; first birds evolved.
Mesozoic Cretaceous 145 - 66 Flowering plants appeared; T. rex and Triceratops roamed; ended with mass extinction.

Tyrannosaurus Rex

Tyrannosaurus Rex was one of the largest and most powerful carnivorous dinosaurs to ever walk the Earth.

Velociraptor

A swift, intelligent hunter from the late Cretaceous period. They used to hunt in packs with excellent coordination.

Stegosaurus

A herbivorous dinosaur easily recognized by the large, bony plates running along its back and used its spiked tail for defense.

Triceratops

A herbivorous dinosaur recognized by its three horns and large bony frill, fed on tough vegetation with its beak-like mouth.

Brachiosaurus

A gentle giant with long neck and towering height, reaching treetops for food. Its front legs were longer than its hind legs.

Ankylosaurus

A heavily armored dinosaur with a massive tail club used for defense. It was a gentle plant-eater that thrived in Cretaceous era.
